Peshkaar Classics (Season 01)

Announced on 1st Baisakh 2073 (13th April 2016), Peshkaar Classics is a series of North Indian Classical performance sessions featuring selected artists from the industry. Peshkaar will continue its dedication towards production of quality music.

  • S01-E01 - Tarana in Raag Hansadhwani Performance by Jonisha Poudel (Vocal), Bijay Lal Shrestha (Sitar). Accompanied by Tabla Maestro Rabin Lal Shrestha.
